ABOUT US

Established in year 2005, Baba Khushyal Das Vidya Mandir School is located in Urban area of Uttar Pradesh state/ut of India. In Areeng area of Goverdhan block of Mathura District. Area pincode is 281001. School is providing Higher Secondary level education. Medium of instruction is Hindi & English language and School is Co-Educational. Currently schools is being guided by principal/head teacher Mr. Om Prakash School is affiliated with State Board for both Secondary and High Secondary level.
